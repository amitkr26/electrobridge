## Why ElectroBridge Has No Real Competitor

**Gap identified:**
- Naukri, LinkedIn, Indeed — generic, no research fellowship focus
- No platform exists specifically for: JRF + PhD + Govt Research + Private Semiconductor + International Fellowship in ONE place
- DRDO/ISRO/CSIR post individually on their own scattered websites
- Indian researchers waste 3-4 hours daily checking 15+ different websites

**Market size (2026 data):**
- India semiconductor sector: ₹1.6 lakh crore investments committed
- 1 million semiconductor jobs projected by 2026
- UGC NET Electronic Science: thousands of candidates per cycle
- CSIR-GATE JRF applications: tens of thousands annually
- India has ~20% of world's chip designers

**ElectroBridge's unique position:** The ONLY niche aggregator for electronics + semiconductor researchers in India

# PART E: SEO + AEO + GEO TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

## SEO
- Every page: unique title, meta description, H1, canonical URL
- H1 → H2 → H3 hierarchy
- Internal linking: opportunities ↔ org pages ↔ category pages ↔ resource pages
- JSON-LD schema on every page type

### Structured Data
- Homepage: WebSite + SearchAction + FAQPage
- Opportunity detail: JobPosting (Google Jobs)
- Resource pages: Article + BreadcrumbList + FAQPage
- Organization pages: Organization
- News articles: NewsArticle
- Category pages: FAQPage
- About page: WebSite + FAQPage

## AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
Write content that directly answers specific questions:
- "What is the JRF stipend?" → Direct answer first sentence
- Supporting details follow with source citation

## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- `/api/opportunities-feed` — structured JSON for AI crawlers
- llms.txt — guides AI crawlers
- Original resource content (not scraped)
- Schema markup everywhere
