# Product Vision

## Mission

Eliminate the friction of discovering opportunities in the semiconductor, VLSI, electronics, and research ecosystem. Make every opportunity, every learning resource, and every professional connection accessible from a single platform — with zero barriers to entry.

## Vision Statement

A world where any student, researcher, or professional in the semiconductor and electronics ecosystem can:
- Discover every relevant opportunity without visiting dozens of websites
- Learn industry-relevant skills for free
- Connect with peers, mentors, and employers in their domain
- Track their career progression from student to industry leader

All without being locked behind paywalls, login walls, or advertising.

## Strategic Goals

### Year 1 (Current)
- Build the world's most comprehensive aggregator of semiconductor/VLSI/research opportunities
- Establish the academy as the go-to free learning resource for VLSI careers
- Achieve product-market fit with the registered user tier

### Year 2
- Scale to 1000+ authenticated scrape sources globally
- AI-powered personalized recommendations and matching
- Employer/recruiter portal as a revenue channel (optional, non-intrusive)

### Year 3
- Mobile native applications
- API platform for third-party integrations
- Community-driven content and moderation

## Non-Goals

- BerojgarDegreeWala is NOT an applicant tracking system
- BerojgarDegreeWala is NOT a general-purpose job board (it is semiconductor/VLSI/electronics specific)
- BerojgarDegreeWala does NOT charge users for access to opportunities or learning
- BerojgarDegreeWala does NOT show ads
- BerojgarDegreeWala does NOT require login to browse opportunities
